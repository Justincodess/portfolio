About

Craft lives in
the details.

I'm Justin — a designer and developer from Kathmandu. I care about clean visual systems, purposeful interfaces, and work that communicates clearly without excess. My practice sits at the intersection of graphic design and frontend development, with a growing curiosity for how AI shapes the tools we build with.

When I'm not designing, I'm building small software tools, reading about typography, or exploring the visual culture that shapes how we see things.
